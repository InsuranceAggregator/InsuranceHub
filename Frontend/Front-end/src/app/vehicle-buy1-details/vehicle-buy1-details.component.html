Description of vehicle insurance
When you purchase vehicle insurance, you enter into a contract with an insurance provider. In exchange for regular premium payments, the insurance company agrees to provide coverage for certain risks associated with owning and operating a vehicle.The coverage provided by vehicle insurance typically includes liability coverage, which protects you against bodily injury and property damage that you may cause to others. It also includes collision and comprehensive coverage, which cover damage to your own vehicle from accidents, theft, vandalism, and other non-collision incidents
